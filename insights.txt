6.  SOC Operational Observations

-   Escalation Levels are evenly distributed, indicating consistent
    multi-tier handling.
-   Open incidents remain high, indicating need for improved response
    automation.
-   Critical Resolution Time remains elevated compared to overall

with high criticality exposure across core infrastructure systems. While
detection time is efficient, response time and escalation rate indicate
operational strain. Enhanced automation, stronger credential management,
and firewall hardening are recommended.
